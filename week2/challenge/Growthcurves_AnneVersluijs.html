1 Introduction

The data that we are going to use for this exercise is published in the mBio paper (Niggli S 2023). It can be found online on: https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.23578086.v1 It describes the OD600 (growth) of the Staphylococcus aureus strain JE2 in different media.
